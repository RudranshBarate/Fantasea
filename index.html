About Us

Welcome to Fantasea

Where Luxury Meets Adventure on Goa’s Waters

At Fantasea, we are dedicated to creating exceptional luxury yacht experiences along the stunning waters of Goa. As a premier yacht charter company, we offer tailor-made cruises that blend comfort, adventure, and elegance.


Our flagship vessel, the Catamaran Yacht Fantasea, is designed to deliver the perfect escape—whether you're celebrating a birthday, anniversary, proposal, private party, or simply seeking a serene retreat. With a capacity to host up to 24 guests, our yacht is equipped with premium amenities and offers breathtaking views of Goa's rivers and coastline.


From tranquil sunset cruises to thrilling water sports, Fantasea curates every journey to match your desires, ensuring memories that last a lifetime
